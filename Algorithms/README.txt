Process-Painting Algorithm(s):
==============================
A. Choosing Subject(s), Theme(s), Medium, Format(s);
B. Prepare Materials and Workspace;
C. Design First Treatment; Always the PRIMING.
D. Check Reference Materials, Archive for Prior Art;
E. Innovate: Design New Treatments always stopping for the Long Tail of Analysis, 
i.e. ArtBursts & Long Tail of Contemplation & Analysis;
F. Stick in Archives, Repeat endlessly.
